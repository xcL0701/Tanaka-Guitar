History About Tanaka Guitar

I used to be (the shop owner) just a music fan, my idol was Ed Sheeran who I think is the greatest guitar player and I admire the most. Until finally he had his own guitar and started to learn the guitar self-taught until he played it fluently.


And I saw an Indonesian singer named Sheryl Sheinafia who has a good guitar unlike usual, so I finally got interested in making my own guitar.


And coincidentally I have the capital to open a shop, I finally made this shop I named Tanaka Guitar. And finally I created a team that is currently the Founder and Co-Founder of this store.
